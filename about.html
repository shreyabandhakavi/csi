ABOUT PLANTS


Plant a tree, plant a life
Planting trees is a very good activity for everyone. Plants play an important role in improving the total living of this planet.
Plants give life to the living organisms. Plants maintain a balance between oxygen and carbon dioxide which is important for the life of fauna.
Nowadays our environment becomes polluted because of vehicles. So we have to plant more trees alongside roads it will help our environment. Planting more trees is very useful in removing heavy metals from contaminated sites.
Plants are helpful for maintaining the groundwater level. All the living organisms depend on plants for their food so plants are considered to be as our lives we can protect them they will protect us.
